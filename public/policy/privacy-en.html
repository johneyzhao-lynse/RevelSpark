II. How We Collect and Use Your Personal Information

As a data controller, we collect and use your personal information in accordance with the principles of lawfulness, fairness, necessity, and good faith for the following purposes.

1. Personal Information You Provide

The Lynse App is classified as a "utility" application. To provide features such as account registration, device pairing, transcription, and summarization, we only require the basic and necessary personal information associated with each feature, except where specific laws and regulations require otherwise. If you refuse to provide such information, we may be unable to provide the corresponding features.

Please note: If you provide personal information belonging to others, you must ensure you have obtained the relevant individuals' consent.

1.1 App Runtime

(1) Necessary and Non-Necessary Information

When you use the App, we collect your network type, IP address, operating system type, app process name, operating system version, and device resolution to verify whether you are a real human user, assess account risk, perform identity verification, and detect and prevent security incidents. This information is necessary for this feature. If you do not agree, you will not be able to use the App normally.

1.2 Account Registration and Login

(1) Necessary Information

To ensure the security of your personal space, you must register and log in to a Lynse account to use features such as voice-to-text transcription, device pairing, and purchasing subscriptions. During registration, you need to provide your phone number and enter an SMS verification code for identity verification. This information is necessary. If you do not provide it, you will not be able to complete registration/login.

1.3 Profile Management

(1) Necessary and Non-Necessary Information

You may choose to modify your nickname, take a photo for your avatar, or select an image from your photo library. We collect your nickname and image information. This is non-necessary information. If you do not provide it, it will not affect your ability to register, log in, or use other Product features.

(2) Permissions

If you choose to use the camera or photo library to upload images, we need to access your camera permission (read), photo library permission (read), or local file access permission. If you deny these permissions, you will not be able to upload images, but it will not affect your use of transcription, summarization, or viewing, editing, and sharing features.

1.4 Adding Devices

(1) Necessary and Non-Necessary Information

To provide the device pairing feature, we collect the device's serial number (SN), MAC address, Wi-Fi SSID, and specific sensor list (Bluetooth) for pairing. This is necessary information for the device pairing feature. After pairing, we only transmit data via Bluetooth when you actively enable recording and file transfer. Bluetooth data transmission is non-necessary information for the device pairing feature.

(2) Permissions

When adding a device, we need to access your Bluetooth permission and location permission (only for Android 12 and below). If you deny these permissions, you will not be able to add devices.

1.5 Subscription Management and Payments

When you purchase subscription services or transcription credits, we collect your order information (including subscription/order type, order number, order source, user ID, order price, actual payment amount, payment channel, payment method, order creation time, and order payment time). This information is necessary for us to confirm your transaction, activate your benefits, and handle potential refunds or customer service issues. If you do not agree, you will not be able to complete subscriptions or payments.

1.6 Transcription and Summarization

(1) Necessary and Non-Necessary Information

When you use the transcription and summarization feature, we collect audio/video files you actively select from your device, or voice/audio files recorded by your Lynse device and synced to the Product. We also collect the text information generated after transcription and analysis. This is necessary information. If you do not provide it, you will not be able to use the transcription and summarization feature.

You may optionally insert photos from your camera or photo library. We collect your image information. This is non-necessary information.

You may import audio files or export audio, transcription results, and summaries. We collect your device's SD card directory information to facilitate file transfers. This is necessary information.

(2) Permissions

To upload local audio for transcription, we need storage permission (read). If you deny it, you will not be able to upload files for transcription.

To insert photos, we need camera permission (read) or photo library permission (read). If you deny these, you will not be able to insert photos.

After transcription is complete, we use notification permission to send you status updates. If you deny this, you will not receive notifications.

To export content, we need photo library permission (write), storage permission (write), or download permission. If you deny these, you will not be able to use the export feature.

To use the sharing feature, we need app list access permission to identify shareable target apps. If you deny this, you will not be able to use the sharing feature.

1.7 User Settings and Preferences

You may set transcription language, industry preferences, and custom vocabulary. We collect your language selection, industry preference, and custom vocabulary. This is non-necessary information.

1.8 Template Community

We offer a template community where you can use public templates or submit your own. To use the photo-to-template feature, we need camera permission (for taking photos) or photo library permission (for selecting images). These permissions are used solely for uploading images to create templates. Uploaded images are not stored on our servers. If you deny these permissions, you will not be able to use the photo-to-template feature, but other community features remain available.

1.9 Clipboard

When you actively copy or paste text within the Product, we need clipboard permission. We do not read or upload your clipboard information to our servers. If you deny this permission, you will not be able to copy/paste quickly, but manual text input remains available.

1.10 Biometric Unlock

The Product offers biometric unlock (fingerprint, face recognition). When enabled, we need biometric authentication permission. This is used solely for local biometric verification. We do not upload your biometric data to our servers or collect/store any personal information unrelated to this feature. If you deny this permission, you will not be able to use biometric unlock, but other authentication methods remain available.

1.11 Help and Support

The "Help & Support" feature offers the following contact methods:

  1. Phone support: When you choose to call our support hotline, we need phone dialing permission. We do not collect your call history, contacts, or store dialing data. If you deny this permission, you cannot dial directly from the app, but you can still contact support through other means.

  2. Enterprise WeChat: When you save a customer service QR code, we need photo library permission (write). We do not read other images in your library. If you deny this permission, you cannot save the QR code, but you can still contact support.

  3. You may export app logs for troubleshooting. We need storage permission (write) to save logs. If you deny this, you cannot export logs, which may affect our ability to troubleshoot issues.

1.12 Experience Improvement Program

With your consent, we may use data you input/provide, instructions you give, summaries and responses generated by the Product, and your usage patterns — after security encryption and de-identification — for internal offline analysis to improve user experience and service optimization. You may opt out at any time through Me > Settings > Preferences > Experience Improvement Program. Opting out will stop new data from being used for analysis but will not affect data already processed under your prior authorization. Opting out does not affect your use of core Product features.
